About

PolarSOL is a liquid staking token (LST) operated by a seasoned validator who actively participates in and contributes to the Solana ecosystem. We don’t just run nodes — we use products, provide feedback, and shape the future of DeFi.

Why PolarSOL

Consistently high APY among Solana LSTs. Built by DeFi-native validator who cares about the network.

Integrity First

We run only unmodified/unpatched validator Jito client, strictly follow Solana’s consensus rules, and do not engage in sandwich attacks or any other unethical practices.

Builder Mindset

We give feedback, propose strategies, and actively improve Solana DEFI ecosystem — not just operate infrastructure.

Decentralized Trust

The LST was created in close collaboration with Sanctum team. The upgrade authority of Sanctum’s LSTs is currently held by a 10-member multisig. All members are highly reputable actors in the space: Jito, Jupiter, Laine, Mango, MRGN, Solblaze, and Sanctum.

Shared Values

We act independently but not in isolation. We regularly consult our community and take into account the voice of our stakers before making governance decisions. Decentralization works best when values are aligned.
